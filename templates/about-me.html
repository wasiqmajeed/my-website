Knowing me of a personal level

Hello,
I am glad that you made it to this part of the Website. Nice to see you here.
My Name is Wasiq Wani. I come from a small city in India called Srinagar which is in the state of Jammu and Kashmir. I was born in Srinagar on 11th December 1997. I also did majority of my schooling from Srinagar. I graduated from my high school in March 2016 and moved to Poland in October 2016 to start my Computer Engineering Course in Vistula University, Warsaw. I graduated from the university in October 2020 and started working full-time after that.

Programming

I can recall my first experience with programming being my younger brother teaching me how to print "Hello world" on the console with C. It looked very interesting and made me more curious about the subject. It was what motivated me to study Computer Engineering instead of Medicine.
The first programming language that I properly learned was C# in university for first two semesters. In the following semesters, I also gained experience with Java and Python. Since the last two years, I have been trying to get better in Python. I have spent quite a bit of time getting familiar with different data structures and algorithms, also getting experience with Flask framework, working with REST API's and implementing SQL with Python.

Hobbies

There are multiple things that I enjoy doing when I am not working or studying. I have been into sports as far as I can remember, I used to play cricket as a child and in my teenage years I was a part of the U-14 and U-19 semi-professional football team which was under the national football association. I am an avid football fan being a big supporter of Real Madrid and Cristiano Ronaldo.

My passion for travelling possibly comes because of my parents who love to travel. My favorite destination to travel was Bali and my next favourite destination to visit is the Caribbean.
